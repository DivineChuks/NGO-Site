SSEIA-In-Schools

Students in school can be given an opportunity to learn and understand STEM Education which is designed to prepare them to play an active role in science and space travels in the coming future. Our team provide support ranging from resources, curriculum, mentors and tools to schools.

Donate for SSEIA Projects
